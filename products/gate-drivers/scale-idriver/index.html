SCALE-iDriver鈩?Series FAQs

Q: What is FluxLink鈩?isolation in SCALE-iDriver?

A: FluxLink is Power Integrations' proprietary galvanic isolation technology that provides a highly reliable and robust isolation barrier, ensuring safe operation between the primary and secondary sides of the gate driver.

Q: Can SCALE-iDriver ICs be used for SiC MOSFETs?

A: Yes, many SCALE-iDriver devices are optimized for driving both IGBTs and SiC MOSFETs, offering the necessary drive strength and protection features for these advanced power switches.

Q: What is CMTI and why is it important for gate drivers?

A: CMTI (Common-Mode Transient Immunity) refers to a gate driver's ability to reject high-frequency noise that can occur across the isolation barrier. High CMTI is crucial in power converter applications to prevent false triggering and ensure reliable operation.
