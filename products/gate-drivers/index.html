Power Integrations SCALE™ Gate Drivers

The Power Integrations SCALE™ family of gate drivers are advanced, galvanically isolated solutions designed for driving IGBTs and SiC MOSFETs in power conversion applications. These drivers provide reliable switching performance, enhanced protection features, and high noise immunity, making them ideal for industrial drives, renewable energy inverters, and other high-power applications.

SCALE drivers utilize advanced magnetic isolation technology to provide reliable operation in harsh electrical environments. The series includes both plug-in and integrated solutions, with features like DESAT protection for IGBTs, active Miller clamp, and programmable switching characteristics. LiTong Group, as an authorized PI distributor, provides comprehensive support for selecting and implementing SCALE driver solutions.

The SCALE series stands out for its exceptional reliability in demanding applications, with options for both standard and intelligent gate driving. The plug-in technology allows for easy replacement and reduces maintenance costs while ensuring consistent performance across different applications.

SCALE™ Series FAQs

Q: What is the difference between SCALE-2 and SCALE-iFlex?

A: SCALE-2 drivers are plug-in solutions with fixed characteristics optimized for specific power modules, while SCALE-iFlex is a flexible, configurable platform that can be adapted to various power modules through configuration files.

Q: Can SCALE drivers be used with SiC MOSFETs?

A: Yes, several SCALE drivers are specifically optimized for SiC MOSFETs, with appropriate gate drive voltages and fast switching characteristics that take advantage of SiC technology.

Q: What is DESAT protection?

A: DESAT (Desaturation) protection is a feature that detects short-circuit or overcurrent conditions in IGBTs by monitoring the voltage across the IGBT when it should be conducting. If a fault is detected, the driver immediately turns off the IGBT safely.
