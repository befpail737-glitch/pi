Power Integrations SCALE-iFlex鈩?Series

The Power Integrations SCALE-iFlex鈩?gate driver system is a highly flexible, modular solution designed for high-power IGBT and SiC MOSFET modules. It offers unparalleled scalability and ease of integration, making it ideal for demanding applications such as high-power motor drives, renewable energy systems, and traction inverters.

SCALE-iFlex systems consist of a central master controller and up to six module-specific gate driver boards, allowing for precise control and protection of large power modules. Key features include advanced active clamping, short-circuit protection, reinforced isolation, and comprehensive diagnostics. This modular approach simplifies the design of complex power converter systems, reduces development time, and enhances overall system reliability. LiTong Group, as an authorized PI distributor, provides expert guidance and support for implementing SCALE-iFlex solutions.

The flexibility of the SCALE-iFlex system allows designers to easily adapt to different power module configurations and power levels, providing a future-proof solution for evolving power electronics requirements. Its robust design ensures reliable operation even in harsh industrial environments, making it a preferred choice for high-power applications.
