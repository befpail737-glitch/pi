AC-DC Conversion Products

LiTong Group offers a comprehensive range of Power Integrations' AC-DC conversion ICs, designed for high efficiency, reliability, and compact power supply solutions. Our portfolio includes industry-leading series such as InnoSwitch, LinkSwitch, and TOPSwitch, catering to a wide array of applications from consumer electronics to industrial power supplies.
