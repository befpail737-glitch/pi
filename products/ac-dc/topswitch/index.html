Power Integrations TOPSwitch™ Series

The Power Integrations TOPSwitch™ family of ICs are off-line switcher devices that integrate a 700 V or 1000 V power MOSFET with a controller in an electrically isolated TO-220 package. These devices dramatically simplify the design of offline power converters by integrating the power MOSFET, PWM controller, thermal shutdown, fault protection, and other necessary functions into a single package.

TOPSwitch devices use an innovative design architecture that eliminates the need for a bias winding or external start-up components. The device operates in discontinuous conduction mode (DCM) and incorporates numerous protection features including thermal shutdown, input overvoltage protection, automatic fault recovery, and safe-restart. They are ideal for applications requiring a compact design with minimal external components, such as appliance controls, industrial power supplies, and consumer electronics. LiTong Group, as an authorized PI distributor, provides comprehensive support for selecting and implementing TOPSwitch solutions.

The TOPSwitch series stands out for its excellent performance in challenging environments due to its high-voltage capability, integrated protection features, and reliable operation. The series includes multiple families optimized for different power levels and applications, from low-power consumer adapters to robust industrial power supplies.

TOPSwitch™ Series FAQs

Q: What is the difference between TOPSwitch-HX and TOPSwitch-JX?

A: TOPSwitch-JX provides additional features compared to TOPSwitch-HX, including adjustable current limit, enhanced line voltage compensation, and extended power range capabilities. TOPSwitch-JX devices also offer better performance at light loads.

Q: Can TOPSwitch devices be used in safety-critical applications?

A: Yes, TOPSwitch devices in TO-220 packages provide electrical isolation between the power MOSFET drain and the heat sink, making them suitable for safety-critical applications. The internal protection features further enhance system safety.

Q: What is the typical efficiency of TOPSwitch devices?

A: Efficiency varies by application and design, but TOPSwitch devices typically achieve 80-85% efficiency in well-designed applications due to their optimized control algorithms and minimal losses.
