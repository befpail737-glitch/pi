Power Integrations LinkSwitch™ Series

The Power Integrations LinkSwitch™ family of ICs are off-line switcher ICs designed for low-power, non-isolated applications. These devices integrate a high-voltage power MOSFET, oscillator, high-voltage switching current source, sense, and start-up functions in a monolithic device. The LinkSwitch series provides constant current regulation with excellent accuracy and minimal component count.

LinkSwitch devices utilize a unique variable frequency, continuous conduction mode (CCM) control that provides excellent line and load regulation while maintaining tight control of component stress under line and load transients. They are ideal for applications requiring a constant current output, such as LED drivers, battery chargers, and power supplies for industrial applications. LiTong Group, as an authorized PI distributor, provides comprehensive support for selecting and implementing LinkSwitch solutions.

The LinkSwitch series stands out for its excellent efficiency at light loads due to auto-restart and frequency adjustment features, integrated soft-start for reduced inrush current, and comprehensive protection including auto-recovery for overvoltage, overtemperature, and short-circuit conditions.

LinkSwitch™ Series FAQs

Q: What is the difference between LinkSwitch-TN3 and LinkSwitch-CP?

A: LinkSwitch-TN3 devices are optimized for constant current applications with integrated bridge rectifiers, while LinkSwitch-CP devices are designed for constant power applications with primary-side regulation and integrated feedback.

Q: Can LinkSwitch devices be used for LED driver applications?

A: Absolutely. The LinkSwitch-TN series is specifically designed for LED driver applications, providing constant current output with excellent stability and dimming capability.

Q: What is the typical efficiency of LinkSwitch devices?

A: Efficiency varies by application and design, but LinkSwitch devices typically achieve 75-85% efficiency in LED driver applications due to their CCM operation and integrated features.
