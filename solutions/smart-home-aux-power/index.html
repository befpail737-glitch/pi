Smart Home Auxiliary Power Solution FAQs

Q: How do these solutions reduce standby power?

A: Our Eco-mode technology automatically adjusts switching frequency at light loads, significantly reducing switching losses and standby power consumption.

Q: Can these solutions support universal input?

A: Yes, all our smart home power solutions support universal input voltage ranges (90-265 VAC) for global deployment.

Q: Are these solutions suitable for battery backup systems?

A: Yes, these solutions are ideal for smart home devices that may have battery backup or UPS integration requirements.
