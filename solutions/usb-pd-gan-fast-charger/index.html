USB PD GaN Fast Charger Solution FAQs

Q: What is the advantage of ZVS in USB PD chargers?

A: Zero-voltage switching significantly reduces switching losses, especially at high frequencies, enabling higher efficiency and higher power density.

Q: How does GaN technology improve charger performance?

A: GaN devices have lower switching and conduction losses than silicon, enabling higher frequency operation and smaller magnetic components.

Q: Can these solutions achieve universal input?

A: Yes, all our USB PD solutions support universal input (90-265VAC) for global compatibility.
