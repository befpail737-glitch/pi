Power Integrations EV Traction Inverter Drive Solution

Power Integrations' EV Traction Inverter Drive solution provides a comprehensive approach to electric vehicle powertrain systems, combining high-performance gate drivers with advanced power conversion technology. Our solution addresses the critical requirements of EV traction inverters: high efficiency, reliability, compact size, and robust protection in demanding automotive environments.

EV Traction Inverter Solution FAQs

Q: Are these solutions AEC-Q100 qualified?

A: Yes, Power Integrations offers AEC-Q100 qualified gate drivers specifically designed for automotive applications, including traction inverters.

Q: What voltage isolation do these drivers provide?

A: Our automotive gate drivers typically provide 5kV isolation or higher, ensuring safety in high-voltage traction applications.

Q: Can these drivers work with both IGBTs and SiC MOSFETs?

A: Yes, we offer drivers optimized for both IGBTs and SiC MOSFETs, with appropriate gate drive voltage and switching characteristics for each technology.
