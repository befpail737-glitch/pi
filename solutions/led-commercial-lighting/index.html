Power Integrations LED Commercial Lighting Solution

Power Integrations' LED Commercial Lighting solution provides highly efficient and reliable power conversion for commercial LED lighting applications. Our solution addresses the specific requirements of commercial environments: high efficiency, excellent power factor, low THD, long lifetime, and compliance with lighting regulations.

The solution leverages our LinkSwitch-PH, LYTSwitch, and other specialized LED driver ICs optimized for commercial lighting applications. These devices provide constant current output for consistent LED performance, with features such as high power factor, low total harmonic distortion, and excellent efficiency across the entire load range. The solution includes options for dimming compatibility and flicker-free operation.

Our LED commercial lighting solution stands out for its exceptional efficiency and reliability in 24/7 commercial applications. The solution enables designers to achieve lighting systems that meet Energy Star and other efficiency standards while providing the long lifetime and reliability required in commercial installations. LiTong Group, as an authorized PI distributor, provides comprehensive support for implementing LED commercial lighting solutions.

LED Commercial Lighting Solution FAQs

Q: How do these solutions achieve high power factor?

A: Our solutions use continuous conduction mode (CCM) control with internal PFC, achieving power factors >0.9 without additional components.

Q: Are these solutions suitable for 24/7 operation?

A>Yes, our solutions are designed for reliability in commercial applications with 24/7 operation and high-temperature environments.

Q: Can these solutions support dimming?

A: Yes, many of our LED driver solutions include dimming support with flicker-free performance across the entire dimming range.
