Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions we receive. If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Q: What is LiTong Group's relationship with Power Integrations?

A: LiTong Group is an officially authorized primary distributor for Power Integrations (PI) products. We provide genuine components and comprehensive support.

Q: How can I get a quote for Power Integrations products?

A: You can request a quote by visiting our Contact Us page and filling out the inquiry form, or by contacting our sales team directly via email or phone.

Q: Do you provide technical support for PI products?

A: Yes, we have a dedicated team of Field Application Engineers (FAEs) who provide expert technical support, design assistance, and guidance on using PI Expert software. Visit our Technical Support section for more resources.

Q: Can I request samples of Power Integrations ICs?

A: Yes, sample requests can be made through our Contact Us page or by reaching out to your regional sales representative.

Q: What is your global sales network?

A: LiTong Group has an extensive global sales network with offices in Greater China, Americas, Europe, and Asia-Pacific regions. You can find details on our Contact Us page.

Still have questions?

We're here to help. Reach out to our team for personalized assistance.
